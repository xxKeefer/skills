# Kaizen

Continuous tiny improvement. 1% better, every day. Experiments graduate to Standards once proven.
Recurring friction triggers a 5 Whys. Failing habits get kaizen-shrunk to a near-zero floor.

## Standards

Locked-in baselines carried into every sprint by default. A row appears here only after an
experiment has been kept across `/ponder`. Standards are subject to calibration, not negotiation.

| Standard | Domain | Adopted | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| e.g. "Close laptop by 21:00" | #domain/AREA | YYYY-MM-DD | [[YYYY-wkNN-ponder]] | optional context |

## Improvement Log

Dated record of one tiny improvement observed per `/reflect`. Append-only. Reviewed weekly in
`/ponder` and summarised by `/chronicle`.

| Date | Domain | Tiny improvement | Triggered by |
|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | #domain/AREA | one-line description | morning intent / friction / serendipity |

## Active Calibrations

Adjustments to a standard or habit when the current floor is too high. Each row records the
shrink, the reason, and the trial sprint.

| Calibration | Standard / Habit | Reason | Trial Sprint | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| e.g. "Shrink run target 5km -> 1km" | "Run 3x/week" | failed 2 sprints | [[YYYY-wkNN-sprint]] | 🔄 |
